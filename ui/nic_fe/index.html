About

Many common causes of unintentional injuries occur in homes due to unsafe housekeeping arising from the lack of information. Our mobile application aims to help families in developing countries identify potential house hazards to create a safe, resilient and sustainable environment for them. By keeping them informed of dangerous hazards, our users will be equipped with the necessary knowledge on safe housekeeping, improving their health and well-being in the long run. We hope to address the following Sustainable Development Goals with our application.
